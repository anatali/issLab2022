RobotExplorer¶

The robot:

  • works as an explorer of the room. Starting from its HOME, the goal is to create a map of the room that records the position of the fixed obstacles;

  • returns immediately to the HOME as soon as it finds an obstacle;

  • stops for a while (e.g. 2 seconds), when it ‘feels’ that the sonar has detected its presence;
